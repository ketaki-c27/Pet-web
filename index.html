About the app

Pet Secure Accord is built for pet owners who want better visibility into their pets, their connected trackers, and the health of their monitoring setup. The app combines tracking, alerts, account management, and payment flows in a single mobile experience.

TR

Tracking

View live pet locations, browse history, and monitor activity, signal, and battery data from connected devices.

IO

Connectivity

The app supports Bluetooth Low Energy connectivity and MQTT-based data streaming so device updates stay responsive and useful.

AC

Account and billing

Users can sign in securely, manage pet profiles, and access paid plans backed by third-party payment handling.

Privacy Policy
How we collect, use, and protect information

Information we collect

  • We may collect your name, email address, login method, profile details, and connected device information.
  • Pet-related details such as pet name, breed, age, weight, activity, and tracker status may be stored to power the app experience.
  • Payment transactions are handled by secure third-party providers such as Razorpay. We do not store full card information or PII inside the app.

How we use information

  • To create and manage your account, sync your pets and devices, and keep your subscription active.
  • To send critical notifications such as geofence alerts, low battery warnings, billing confirmations, and support replies.
  • To improve app reliability, troubleshoot issues, and understand which features are most helpful to pet owners.

How we share information

  • We may share limited data with service providers that help us deliver authentication, cloud storage, notifications, analytics, and payments.
  • We may disclose information when required by law, to protect user safety, or to investigate fraud and misuse.
  • We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

Data retention and security

  • We retain data only for as long as it is needed for account operations, compliance, dispute handling, or legitimate business needs.
  • We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.
  • You can request updates or deletion of profile information by contacting support through the app's help channels.

Refund Policy
How refund requests are reviewed and processed

Eligible refund scenarios

  • You may request a refund for duplicate payments, accidental double charges, or charges caused by a clear technical error.
  • Refunds may also be considered when a paid plan was charged but not activated within a reasonable time after successful payment.
  • Unauthorized transactions should be reported immediately so they can be reviewed with the payment provider.

Non-refundable situations

  • Completed billing cycles, partial plan usage, or dissatisfaction with features alone may not qualify for a refund.
  • Delays caused by bank processing, card issuer checks, or network interruptions are not automatically refundable.
  • Promotional, discounted, or trial conversion payments may be non-refundable where clearly disclosed before purchase.

How to request a refund

  • Contact support with your registered email address, transaction reference, payment date, and the reason for your request.
  • Include screenshots or receipts whenever possible to speed up verification.
  • Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method and may take additional banking time to appear.

Review timeline

  • Refund requests should be submitted as soon as possible after the charge appears.
  • Our team will review each request individually and may ask for extra details before making a decision.
  • If a refund is denied, we will share the reason and, when possible, suggest the next best support path.
